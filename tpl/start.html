Women's
Wellbeing Project

Do you worry about whether your relationship is healthy?
Do you sometimes wonder if you are safe?
If you are an Australian woman aged between 16 and 50 and you have experienced relationship issues over the last 6 months you are invited to take part in this project.
BE A PART OF THE PROJECT

THE TEAM

We are a team of researchers in general practice, social work, women's health, nursing, and health technology. The project is led by The University of Melbourne.

THE PROJECT

Many women in Australia feel unsafe or fearful or worry that their relationships with their partners are not healthy. We are testing a website for women to self-reflect on the health of their relationhips, become more informed about what might help them, and plan for their safety and well being.

"Learn to let go of what you can't change and take control of what you can." - iMatter
